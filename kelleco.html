Kellé Company

Print Design

Kellé Company is a leader in the performance dancewear industry. They design and manufacture apparel for dance recitals and competitions. I am the lead chorographer and on set design assistant for all catalog production and print design. Below you will see some of our amazing products and work.

View 2021 Catalog

For the past 10 years I have had the opportunity to play an essential, lead role in catalog and photoshoot production at Kellé Co. On set I work directly with the director and CEO while acting as the liaison for communication between the models, photo team and styling.

The Work

The goal of my job is to make the product look amazing, while creating beautiful shapes and movement with my choreography. I strive to create an inclusive and supportive environment that brings the energy on set to life.
