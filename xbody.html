X Body

Product Design

X Body is a dance and fitness wear line by the international company, Dream Duffel, created with a dancer-first focus that is a wardrobe must-have. Made by a team of designers who are dancers and dance educators themselves, the line of dance fundamentals - bras, leggings, joggers, tanks and tees - will cover every player in the business, including teachers and parents. I was the lead designer, sourcing, concepting and leading the production design and development.

View X Body Line

With my extensive background in dance education, the X Body line was inspired by Barteniff Fundamentals, which posits that all movement stems from the body positioned as an "X", and that by sequencing thought the body, we can move with ease and in harmony.
